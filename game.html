GAME

Game, a subsidiary of Massmart Holdings, is a discount retailer of general merchandise and non-perishable groceries. Game was founded in 1970 in Durban on the principle of making shopping a pleasant experience. Today there are 110 stores in 12 countries with the headquarters in Sandton, South Africa. These stores are located across the country including stores in Cape Town, Port Elizabeth, Johannesburg, Durban, and more locations. The many products available at Game include electrical and home appliances, mobiles and computers, baby products, sporting equipment, DIY and auto accessories, household and gardening items, health and beauty products, and liquor.
